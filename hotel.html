HOTEL YAK AND YETI

Hotel Yak and Yeti is a 5-star deluxe hotel in the heart of Kathmandu, Nepal. Modern day sophistication meets cultural heritage in the ample grounds of this over 100-year-old palace. The entire hotel is a sumptuous array of beautiful artefacts, art & traditionally crafted fixtures.

A new wing of an additional 150 rooms was built in harmony with the architectural features of the old-fashionable palace, Lal Durbar, bringing the room total to 270. Attention detail has been meticulously considered throughout, giving a feeling of an art gallery or museum.

The hotel boasts a shopping arcade, a swimming pool, two tennis courts and a state of the art fitness center & luxury spa. At the epicenter of hotel’s rich history, Lal Durbar or Red Palace hosts a grand ballroom, a casino, banquet rooms & famous Chimney restaurant.

Enjoy a feast of beauty & original heritage interiors that have been lovingly maintained at Hotel Yak and Yeti.
